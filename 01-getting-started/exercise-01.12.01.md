# Exercise 1.12.1

> 1. Give a tuple of length `5` where the components are the values `2` and `3`.  
> 2. Give a tuple of type `int × (int × (bool × bool))`.  
> 3. Give a pair whose first component is a pair and whose second component is a triple.

---

### 1.

```ocaml
(2, 2, 3, 3, 3)
```

### 2.

```ocaml
(1, (2, (true, false)))
```

### 3.

```ocaml
((2, 2), (3, 3, 3))
```
