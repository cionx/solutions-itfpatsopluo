# Exercise 4.1.1

> Give the types of the following lists and tuples.
> 1. `[1, 2, 3]`
> 2. `(1, 2, 3)`
> 3. `[(1, 2), (2, 3)]`
> 4. `((1, 2), (2, 3))`
> 5. `[[1, 2], [2, 3]]`

---

We have the following types:

|    | expression         | type                |
|:---|:-------------------|:--------------------|
| 1. | `[1, 2, 3]`        | `L(Z)`              |
| 2. | `(1, 2, 3)`        | `Z × Z × Z`         |
| 3. | `[(1, 2), (2, 3)]` | `L(Z × Z)`          |
| 4. | `((1, 2), (2, 3))` | `(Z × Z) × (Z × Z)` |
| 5. | `[[1, 2], [2, 3]]` | `L(L(Z))`           |
