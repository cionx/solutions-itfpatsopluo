# Exercise 8.7.1

> Give the order of the following functions using big O-notation.
> 1. $\lambda n. \; 2n + 13$
> 2. $\lambda n. \; 5n^2 + 2n + 13$
> 3. $\lambda n. \; 5n^3 + n(n + 1) + 13n + 2$
> 4. $\lambda n. \; 2n + n^4$
> 5. $\lambda n. \; 2^{n + 5} + n^2$

---

The expressions have order of growth $O(n)$, $O(n^2)$, $O(n^3)$, $O(n^4)$ and $O(n^5)$.
